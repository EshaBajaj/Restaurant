Our Gourmet-Specials
Made with only the finest ingredients, our Sev Puri is a true indulgence, elevating the traditional street snack to a luxurious experience. Served with a hint of spice and garnished to perfection, it is the perfect appetizer or a light, refreshing dish to enjoy as part of your gourmet meal at The Indian Kitchen. Our Payasam is a luxurious, soul-soothing dessert that brings the warmth and richness of traditional Indian sweets to your table with a gourmet twist. Made with slow-cooked rice or vermicelli, infused with aromatic spices like cardamom, and sweetened with the finest sugar, this creamy delicacy is a perfect blend of textures and flavors.
